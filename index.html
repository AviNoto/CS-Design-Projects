For this project, I made a playable game inspired by crossy road but very different. You are the hawk trying to avoid the chicken using the arrow keys and collect cars using by clicking. You get one point for clicking on a car and lose two when you collide with the chicken and if you have less than zero points, you lose and the game ends.
